SUPER KINGS ACADEMY

The Super Kings Academy in Dallas, Texas is the first centre of the Super Kings Academy in the USA. The aim of this Academy is to impart the CSK methodology of playing cricket and its philosophy around teaching and learning to the students.

ABOUT SUPER KINGS ACADEMY

The Super Kings Academy is an initiative taken by the Chennai Super Kings to provide world-class coaching and training to both boys and girls of various age groups. The Super Kings Academies came into existence in April 2022 and currently has five in various parts of India (Chennai, Salem, Tirunelveli, Trichy and Hosur) and one in Berkshire, United Kingdom.

The Super Kings Academy believes in the holistic development of the students contributing to their overall wellbeing apart from cricketing skills.
